Questions

Honest answers.

Is it really free?

Yes. CreatorCRM is self-hosted software you run yourself — there's no subscription. It uses the Gmail API (free) and a Cloudflare Worker on the free tier for tracking, so your running cost is genuinely $0.

What's the catch?

You host it yourself. That means a few minutes of setup (connect Gmail, deploy a small worker) instead of a credit card. In exchange you get no fees, no caps beyond Gmail's own, and full ownership of your data.

Do I need to be a developer?

Basic comfort running a command and following a setup guide helps, but the app itself is fully visual — import, compose, and send all happen in the browser.

Is my data safe?

Your contacts live in a local SQLite database on your own machine. Nothing is uploaded to a third-party CRM, and emails send directly through your authenticated Gmail.

How many emails can I send?

As many as Gmail allows: roughly 500/day on a personal account and up to 2,000/day on Google Workspace. CreatorCRM tracks your daily budget so you stay under the limit.

Can I use my normal Gmail?

Yes — personal Gmail or Google Workspace. You authorize once and CreatorCRM sends on your behalf from your own address.
